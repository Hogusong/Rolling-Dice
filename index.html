Game rules

When the player rolls #1, the player will lose the turn without gaining points.

When the player rolls the same number twice in a row, the player will lose the total score.

When the player rolls two #6s, the player will lose the game.

The player will win when the player's total score reaches the target score.

Help

New Game: Start a new game.

Roll Dice: Roll the dice.

Hold: Add current score to the player's score and change the turn.
